A Smaller Animal (Um Animal Menor) is a 20 min short film produced in 2010 by Okna. Pedro Harres and Marcos Contreras are the directors and scriptwriters. The cast of the film consists in Elisa Volpatto and Jorge Júnior.

Synopsis

A woman, a well and a boy. Those three elements shape this fable about power and affection. On the inside, a trapped woman weakened by the situation; on the outside, a boy who has become an obstacle to her freedom.
